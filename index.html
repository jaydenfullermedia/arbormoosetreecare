Who we are

A family business, with a Dutch name and a moose nickname

Mike and Sara Moes started Arbor Moose in January 2019. "Moes" is Dutch — it's pronounced "Moose," and Mike's been called Mr. Moose most of his life, so the name stuck.

Today it's still Mike, Sara, their four kids, and our right-hand man Will working through his arborist apprenticeship. We wanted to build an honest living and an honest business. Seven years in, that's still the whole idea.
