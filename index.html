Frequently asked questions

How do I get a quote? add
Send us a photo of the tree or just your address by text, email, or the contact form. For most jobs we can quote from a photo and get it back to you within 24 hours. Larger or trickier jobs may need a quick on-site look.
Are you certified and insured? add
Yes. Michael Moes is an ISA Certified Arborist (ON-2299A), and we carry full insurance on every job, so you're covered.
Do you have to remove the tree, or can it be saved? add
Removal is always a last resort. Often pruning, cabling or bracing can keep a weakening or older tree healthy and safe. We'll give you honest advice on what's actually best for the tree and your property.
Do you clean up afterward? add
Always. The job isn't finished until your property is left clean and tidy. We can also grind the stump and haul away the wood and debris.
What areas do you serve? add
We're based in St. Catharines and serve the whole Niagara region — including Niagara-on-the-Lake, Niagara Falls, Thorold, Welland, Pelham, Lincoln, Grimsby, Port Colborne and Fort Erie. Not sure if you're in range? Just ask.
Can you help after a storm? add
Yes. Call us for urgent storm damage and we'll help as soon as we safely can — or ride out the storm and let us handle the cleanup afterward.
