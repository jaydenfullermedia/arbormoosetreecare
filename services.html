What we offer

Our services

Certified, local, insured tree care for the Niagara region — the whole job, from the first cut to the last bit of cleanup.

content_cut

Pruning

Maintain your trees' health by removing dead, damaged and dying branches — and reduce hazards to people and structures nearby. Pruning is the first step to a long, healthy life for your trees.

forest

Removals

Dead or dying tree? Renovating, building, or opening up a pool to more sunlight? Removals are a last resort, but sometimes necessary — and a good moment to rethink how a well-placed tree could benefit your property.

grass

Stump Grinding

Remove an unpleasant stump for looks, or just to make your lawn easier to cut and maintain. No problem.

cable

Cabling & Bracing

Want to keep that old, beautiful backyard tree? When limbs weaken or the tree needs support, cabling gives it mechanical help — redistributing weight and stress to reduce the risk of failure.

thunderstorm

Storm Damage

High winds, heavy rain, or ice storms — we're here to help. Call us for immediate help, or ride out the storm and let us handle the cleanup afterwards.

compost

Planting & Fertilization

Adding trees to your oasis? Whether you've already bought them or need help choosing what to plant and where, we'll make the job easier and dig the holes for you. Young trees need the right nutrients to thrive.

lightbulb

Lighting

Sprucing up your landscaping or feeling festive for Christmas? We do the climb for you. You provide the lights — we conquer the heights.

handyman

One-of-a-Kind Jobs

Got a unique job and not sure who to call? We might just be crazy enough to do it — from rescuing cats and drones to installing cameras for bird watching. You won't know until you ask.
